About Us

More Than Just Coffee

TommyCo Coffee was born from a simple dream in 2020 — to create a space where every cup tells a story. But before the coffee shop existed, there was Tommy — a faithful dog who sat so quiet, so comforting, so joyful, so amiable every day.

When Tommy passed, all that was left was the feeling of gratitude and love of the moments spent together. TommyCo Coffee was born in his memory not only as a business, but also as a place that would be as warm as he used to be. It began as a small neighborhood cart in Sta. Ana has developed into a favorite neighborhood where friends get together, ideas are generated and memories are created.

We source our beans directly from local farmers in the Philippines, ensuring fair trade and the freshest roast. Every barista at TommyCo is trained to craft each drink with precision and heart. Our mission is simple: serve exceptional coffee while making everyone feel like family — just like Tommy always did.
